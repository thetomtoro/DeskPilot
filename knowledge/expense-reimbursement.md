# Expense Reimbursement Policy

**Owner:** Finance & Accounting
**Last Updated:** January 2025

---

## Overview

Acme Corp reimburses employees for reasonable, business-related expenses incurred in the performance of their duties. All expenses must be submitted through **Expensify** (expensify.acmecorp.com) within 30 days of the expense date. Expense reports submitted after 60 days require VP approval and may not be reimbursed.

---

## Eligible Expenses

### Business Travel

- Flights: Economy class for domestic travel; business class allowed for flights over 6 hours with VP approval
- Hotel: Up to **$250/night** in standard markets; up to $350/night in high-cost cities (NYC, SF, London, etc.)
- Ground transportation: Rideshare, taxi, public transit — actual cost reimbursed; personal vehicle at **$0.67/mile** (2025 IRS rate)
- Meals while traveling: Up to **$75/day** (breakfast $15, lunch $20, dinner $40) — no alcohol on company card

### Meals & Entertainment

- Team meals (manager-approved): Actual cost, up to **$50/person**
- Client entertainment: Actual cost with prior manager approval; attendee list required in Expensify notes
- Working lunches (3+ people): Up to $25/person

### Office Supplies

- Home office supplies for remote workers: Up to **$50/month** without prior approval
- In-office supplies: Use company procurement via ServiceNow for orders over $30

### Professional Development

- Books, online courses, conferences: Covered under the **$1,000/year professional development budget** per employee
- Conference travel follows the same travel policy above
- Submit PD expenses with a brief note on how it relates to your role

### Software & Subscriptions (Personal)

- Personal software used for work may be reimbursed up to **$20/month** with manager approval. For larger tools, use the software access request process instead.

---

## Expenses That Are NOT Reimbursable

- Alcohol (unless pre-approved for client entertainment)
- Personal travel add-ons (seat upgrades, checked bag fees beyond one bag, travel insurance)
- Traffic tickets, parking fines
- Gym memberships or wellness subscriptions (covered separately under the Wellness Benefit)
- Clothing, unless required safety/PPE gear
- Spouse/partner travel

---

## How to Submit an Expense Report

1. Log in to **Expensify** at expensify.acmecorp.com (SSO via Okta).
2. Click **New Report** and add individual expenses.
3. Attach receipts for all expenses **over $25**. Receipts under $25 are optional but recommended.
4. Add a business justification note for each expense.
5. Tag expenses with the appropriate cost center and project code (ask your manager if unsure).
6. Submit the report — your manager receives an approval notification automatically.

---

## Approval Thresholds

| Report Total | Approver |
|---|---|
| Under $500 | Direct manager |
| $500–$2,000 | Direct manager + department VP |
| Over $2,000 | Direct manager + VP + Finance |

Finance reviews all reports for policy compliance regardless of amount. Non-compliant reports are returned to the employee for correction.

---

## Processing Timeline

Once fully approved, reimbursements are processed in the **next bi-weekly payroll cycle**. Payments appear as a separate line item on your pay stub labeled "Expense Reimbursement." Expect **up to 2 weeks** from final approval to receipt of funds.

International employees are reimbursed in local currency at the exchange rate on the expense date.

---

## Corporate Card Policy

Select employees (managers and above, frequent travelers) are issued an **Acme Corp Visa corporate card**:

- Corporate card expenses must still be submitted in Expensify within 30 days.
- Personal expenses on the corporate card must be flagged and repaid within 15 days.
- Lost or compromised cards: call the number on the back immediately, then notify Finance.
- Corporate cards must be returned upon separation from the company.

To request a corporate card, submit a ServiceNow ticket under **Finance > Corporate Card Request** with manager approval.

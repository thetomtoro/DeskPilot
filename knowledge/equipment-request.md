# IT Equipment Request Process

**Owner:** IT Operations
**Last Updated:** January 2025

---

## Overview

Acme Corp provides employees with the tools they need to do their best work. This document covers how to request standard equipment, upgrade existing hardware, and what policies apply to remote workers.

All equipment requests are submitted via **ServiceNow** at service.acmecorp.com.

---

## Standard Equipment Packages

### Standard Package (All New Hires)

Every new hire receives the following upon joining:

- **Laptop:** 14" MacBook Pro (M3) or Dell XPS 15 (Windows) — based on role/team preference indicated in the onboarding form
- **External monitor:** 27" Dell UltraSharp 4K USB-C monitor
- **Peripherals:** Wireless keyboard, wireless mouse, USB-C hub
- **Headset:** Jabra Evolve2 55 (for customer-facing or frequent-meeting roles) or Poly Voyager (standard)
- **Power adapter:** Compatible with issued laptop

### Engineering/Design Upgrade Package

Engineers and designers may request upgraded specs (16" MacBook Pro M3 Max or equivalent Windows spec). This requires manager approval and is subject to budget availability.

---

## Budget Limits

| Category | Budget Cap |
|---|---|
| New hire equipment (full setup) | $2,500 |
| Hardware refresh (laptop only) | $1,500 |
| Peripheral replacement (single item) | $300 |
| Engineering/Design upgrade package | $3,500 |

Requests exceeding the standard budget cap require **VP-level approval** in addition to manager approval.

---

## How to Request Equipment

### New Hire Equipment

New hire equipment is automatically provisioned by IT based on the start date in Workday. No ticket is needed — your hiring manager should confirm your laptop preference (Mac or Windows) in the onboarding form at least **5 business days before your start date.**

### Additional or Replacement Equipment

1. Go to **service.acmecorp.com**.
2. Select **IT Services > Hardware Request**.
3. Fill in the request form: item needed, business justification, urgency level.
4. Submit — your manager receives an approval request automatically.
5. Once manager-approved, IT reviews and fulfills within **5–10 business days**.

For urgent replacements (e.g., laptop failure), call the IT Help Desk at ext. 4357 for expedited handling.

---

## Refresh Cycle

Acme Corp refreshes hardware on the following schedule:

| Device | Refresh Cycle |
|---|---|
| Laptops | Every 3 years |
| External monitors | Every 5 years |
| Keyboards and mice | On failure or 4 years |
| Headsets | On failure or 3 years |

IT will proactively notify employees when their laptop is due for refresh via email. Employees can also check their current equipment age in ServiceNow under **My Assets**.

---

## Remote Worker Equipment Policy

Remote employees are eligible for the same standard equipment packages as in-office employees.

- **Shipping:** Equipment is shipped to your home address on file in Workday. Standard shipping takes 5–7 business days; expedited shipping (2 business days) is available for an additional cost approved by IT.
- **Home office stipend:** Full-time remote employees receive a **one-time $500 home office stipend** for furniture/desk accessories, reimbursed through Expensify with receipts.
- **Internet reimbursement:** Remote employees may expense up to **$60/month** toward home internet as a business expense.
- **Equipment return:** All company equipment must be returned within 10 business days of separation. IT will email a prepaid shipping label upon offboarding.

---

## Lost, Stolen, or Damaged Equipment

Report lost or stolen equipment to IT and Security immediately:

1. File a ServiceNow ticket under **IT Services > Lost/Stolen Device**.
2. Contact your manager.
3. For theft, file a police report — IT will request the report number for insurance.

Accidental damage is covered once per 3-year refresh cycle. Repeat incidents or negligence may result in the employee sharing repair costs.

# New Employee Onboarding Checklist

**Owner:** People Operations & IT
**Last Updated:** March 2025

---

## Overview

Welcome to Acme Corp! This checklist guides you through your first 90 days. Your manager and onboarding buddy will support you throughout, but this document is your reference for what to complete and when.

Your onboarding portal is available at **onboarding.acmecorp.com** — all tasks are tracked there.

---

## Week 1 — Getting Set Up

### Day 1 (IT Setup)

- [ ] Receive and power on your company laptop (shipped or handed over at office)
- [ ] Complete initial macOS/Windows setup using the IT Setup Guide (emailed to personal address before start)
- [ ] Connect to **GlobalProtect VPN** (see vpn-setup guide in the knowledge base)
- [ ] Log in to **Okta** (acmecorp.okta.com) and set up MFA with Okta Verify
- [ ] Access **Google Workspace** — confirm company email is working
- [ ] Join **Slack** workspace (invite sent to company email); install desktop app
- [ ] Join required Slack channels: #general, #announcements, #your-team, #new-hires, #random

### Day 1–2 (HR Paperwork)

- [ ] Complete I-9 employment verification (in-person or via remote I-9 service)
- [ ] Submit direct deposit information in **Workday**
- [ ] Enroll in benefits (health, dental, vision, 401k) — you have **30 days** from start date to elect coverage
- [ ] Review and sign the Employee Handbook acknowledgment in Workday
- [ ] Review and sign the Confidentiality & IP Agreement (sent via DocuSign)

### Week 1 (Team & Tools)

- [ ] Meet your **onboarding buddy** (assigned by your manager — they'll reach out Day 1)
- [ ] Schedule 1:1 with your manager (recurring weekly cadence)
- [ ] Schedule intro calls with immediate team members (your manager will share a list)
- [ ] Set up **1Password** and save your initial credentials
- [ ] Access **Jira** and **Confluence** — confirm you can see your team's space
- [ ] Review your team's Confluence space: team norms, architecture docs, current sprint

### Required Trainings (Complete by End of Week 2)

- [ ] **Security Awareness Training** — assigned in KnowBe4 (link in Okta dashboard); ~45 minutes
- [ ] **Code of Conduct Training** — assigned in Workday Learning; ~30 minutes
- [ ] **Harassment Prevention Training** — assigned in Workday Learning; required by law in many states; ~60 minutes

---

## Week 2 — Getting to Work

- [ ] Attend your first **team standup** and sprint planning / retro (as applicable)
- [ ] Complete **codebase walkthrough** with a senior engineer on your team (engineering roles)
- [ ] Set up your **local dev environment** using the README in the main GitHub repo
- [ ] Pick up your **first ticket** — your manager will tag one labeled "good-first-ticket" in Jira
- [ ] Shadow at least one customer call or support session (product/support roles)
- [ ] Complete any role-specific tool training assigned by your manager

---

## 30-Day Milestone

By the end of your first month, you should have:

- Completed all required compliance trainings
- Submitted your first pull request or delivered your first work artifact
- A clear understanding of your team's goals for the current quarter
- Met with your manager for a 30-day check-in (scheduled by your manager)

---

## 60-Day Milestone

By day 60, you should have:

- Completed 2–3 meaningful tickets or projects independently
- Developed familiarity with the team's tech stack, processes, and key stakeholders
- Established working relationships with cross-functional partners
- Shared onboarding feedback in #onboarding-feedback on Slack

---

## 90-Day Milestone

By day 90, you should have:

- Set your **OKRs / performance goals** for the quarter in Workday
- Completed your 90-day check-in with your manager
- Taken ownership of at least one ongoing responsibility
- Completed any remaining role-specific certifications or trainings

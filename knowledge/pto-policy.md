# Acme Corp PTO Policy

**Effective Date:** January 1, 2025
**Owner:** People Operations

---

## Overview

Acme Corp provides paid time off (PTO) to support employee wellbeing and work-life balance. PTO covers vacation, personal days, and planned absences. Sick leave is tracked separately.

---

## Annual PTO Allowance

PTO is based on tenure as of the employee's hire anniversary date:

| Tenure | Annual PTO |
|---|---|
| 0–3 years | 15 days |
| 3–5 years | 20 days |
| 5+ years | 25 days |

New hires begin accruing PTO from their first day of employment. Part-time employees accrue PTO on a prorated basis relative to their scheduled hours.

---

## Accrual Schedule

PTO accrues at a rate of **1.25 days per month** for employees in the 0–3 year tier. Employees in higher tiers accrue at proportionally higher rates to reach their annual totals by year-end. Accruals appear in Workday on the 1st of each month.

---

## Rollover Policy

Employees may carry over a **maximum of 5 unused PTO days** into the following calendar year. Any balance above 5 days is forfeited on December 31. Acme Corp does not pay out unused PTO at year-end except where required by state law (e.g., California). Upon voluntary resignation or termination, accrued and unused PTO is paid out in the final paycheck per applicable state regulations.

---

## How to Request PTO

1. Log in to **Workday** (workday.acmecorp.com).
2. Navigate to **Time Off > Request**.
3. Select the dates and PTO type.
4. Submit for manager approval.

**Notice requirements:**
- 1–2 days: 3 business days notice preferred
- 3 or more consecutive days: **minimum 2 weeks advance notice** required
- Extended leave (10+ days): coordinate with HR and your manager at least 4 weeks ahead

Managers must approve or decline requests within 3 business days. During peak blackout periods (e.g., fiscal year-end in December), PTO requests may be restricted — check with your manager.

---

## Sick Leave

Sick leave is a **separate bank of 10 days per year**, granted in full on January 1 (or pro-rated for new hires). Sick leave does not roll over and is not paid out upon separation. Sick leave may be used for:

- Personal illness or injury
- Medical appointments (self or immediate family)
- Mental health days
- Caring for a sick dependent

If illness extends beyond 5 consecutive days, contact HR to initiate Short-Term Disability leave.

---

## Company Holidays

Acme Corp observes the following **10 paid holidays** per year:

1. New Year's Day (January 1)
2. Martin Luther King Jr. Day (3rd Monday in January)
3. Presidents' Day (3rd Monday in February)
4. Memorial Day (last Monday in May)
5. Independence Day (July 4)
6. Labor Day (1st Monday in September)
7. Thanksgiving Day (4th Thursday in November)
8. Day After Thanksgiving (Friday)
9. Christmas Eve (December 24)
10. Christmas Day (December 25)

When a holiday falls on a weekend, the nearest weekday is observed. Holiday schedules are published in Workday each November for the following year.
